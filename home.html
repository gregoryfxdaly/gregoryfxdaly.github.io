Welcome!

Welcome to the Gregory F.X. Daly web page. I am the Collector of Revenue for the City of St. Louis, Missouri and have just begun my 3rd term in March, 2015. The responsibility of the Collector of Revenue is to collect real estate taxes, personal property taxes, earnings taxes, and water and refuse bills fairly and efficiently. It is my goal to do so in a way that inspires public confidence while simultaneously enforcing the Missouri State Statutes and providing exemplary public service.

By clicking on the menu items above you will be able to learn more about me as an individual and about the office of the Collector of Revenue. If you are interested in joining my team, supporting my campaign or have any questions pertaining to the Collector of Revenue, please don’t hesitate to contact us.

I hope that this web site provides the information you are looking for.

Sincerely,
Gregory F.X. Daly
